My career has a theme: bringing new entertainment concepts to life

Developing the idea, refining the details, raising the capital, building the A+ team... Then going to market, seeing smiles on the faces of millions of real people.

So fun.

I've been fortunate to spend my professional life conceptualizing new location-based entertainment categories and building winning business models around them.

From developing and opening the first escape-room style concept in the world over 20 years ago, to designing & building some of your favorite attractions and exhibits at your most-loved theme parks and museums, to serving dozens of clients and many millions of guests, now leading the way in the emerging Challenge Room entertainment space.

Fun Stuff

In addition to my companies, I've been fortunate to work with quality people and organizations.

  • MIT: Product Design

    For 18 years, I've been on the MIT Faculty, as part of the teaching team for 2.009: The Product Design Process at the Massachuetts Institute of Technology (MIT), my alma mater. This is the capstone class of the mechanical engineering major, as students work in teams to conceptualize a consumer product and build a fully-functional alpha prototype in 3 short months.

    I've also helped teach 2.007, the famous robot competition class. Fun, fun.

  • Act III

    Building LEVEL99, I've had the great pleasure to work with Ron Shaich, founder and former CEO and Chair of Panera Bread and current Chairman and lead investor of Cava, Tatte Bakery, Life Alive Organic Cafe, and Honest Greens.
