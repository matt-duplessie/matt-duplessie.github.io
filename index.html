  • Act3

    In launching LEVEL99, Matt had the great fortune of working with Ron Shaich, whose investment firm Act3 partners with consumer facing brands to help them build dominant positions in the catgories of the future. Act3 is the lead investor backing LEVEL99.

  • MIT: Product Design

    For 18 years, Matt has been on the MIT Faculty, as part of the teaching team for 2.009: The Produce Design Process at the Massachuetts Institute of Technology (MIT), Matt's alma mater. This is the capstone class of the mechanical engineering major, as students work in teams to conceptualize a consumer product and build a fully-functional alpha prototype in 3 short months.

    I also got to help with 2.007, the famous robot competition class. Fun, fun.
