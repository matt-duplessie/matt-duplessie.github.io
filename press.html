Hi, I'm Matt


My career has a theme: bringing new entertainment concepts to life


Developing the idea, refining the details, raising the capital, building the A+ team... Then going to market, seeing smiles on the faces of millions of real people.

So fun.

I've been fortunate to spend my career conceptualizing new location-based entertainment categories and building winning business models around them.

From developing and opening the first escape-room style concept in the world over 20 years ago, to designing & building some of your favorite attractions and exhibits at your favorite theme park and museum, to serving dozens of clients and many millions of guests, now leading the way in the emerging Challenge Room entertainment space.
